Mechanical Keyboard Starter Template

Based on the STM32F303 Microcontroller

This project template is the basis of a mechanical keyboard based on the STM32F303 microcontroller, an ARM Cortex M4 core with FPU and DSP instructions.

This template includes a schematic for RGB LED underglow, ESD protection circuit, per-switch LED support, as well as jumpers for connecting directly to the MCU.
